PhD research (discrete mathematics)

My PhD research fits in the context of the study of graph polynomials. The general goal is to investigate connections between algebraic/analytical properties of graph polynomials of graphs and the combinatorial properties of the graphs. We are also interested in the possible algorithmical applications. So far, I have been mostly working with the characteristic and the matching polynomials of graphs.
